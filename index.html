VST2413 is a software FM synthesizer based on YM2413 (OPLL) emulator. YM2413 is well known as low-cost FM sound chip, and was used on Sega Master System and FM-PAC sound expansion cartridge for MSX.

Photo courtesy of Wikipedia

VST2413 can resemble that warm texture of sound and may be well suited for use in "post 8-bit era style" chiptune music.
